Important Places

Iceland's natural features make it a popular tousrist destination. The main city people visit is the capital city, Reykjavík. Believed to be the first permanent settlements in Iceland. Reykjavík is the center of Iceland's cultural, economic and governmental activity. Reykjavík is known to be one of the greenest, safest cities in the world.

Vatnajökull National Park is a protected wilderness area in Iceland, it is centered around the Vatnajökull glacier. The park covers 14,141 square kilometers, this takes up 14% of the countires total area. The national park includes volcanoes, glaciers, waterfalls, lagoons, mountains and more making it a main travel destination.
