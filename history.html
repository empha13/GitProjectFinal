Iceland History and More!

History

Iceland became an independant country in 1944 after being under Denmark rule for over 500 years. Iceland made history with voting the first female and openly gay Prime Minister, Jóhanna Sigurðardóttir.

Location

Iceland is located in the North Atlantic Ocean. It is situated between Greenland and Norway. There are no countries that technically border Iceland as it is an island, in the native language the name for Iceland is Ísland. Iceland is 102,775KM2 in area.

Date & Time

The time-zone in Iceland is (GMT) Greenwich Mean Time. Newley adapted in 2022, there is no longer daylight-savings time. There is a period of time where it is daylight for 24 hours. During Mid-May - Mid-August the sun only sets for 3 hours per day, they call this Midnight Sun.
